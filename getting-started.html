Getting Started


Requirements

What are the requirements to work as a SupaAgent?
Personal Computer:
You will need to have a personal computer (either laptop or desktop), preferably that is personally yours - do not use any public computers. It is not recommended to work from a tablet or mobile phone as our products - Workplace (project management platform) and Task Console (platform to distribute microtasks) - are not built for it.

Safe, speedy and reliable internet:
You need to have access to safe, speedy and reliable internet. You will need this to work on your projects as well to receive important updates and notifications. It is not recommended to connect to a public network.

Google Chrome:
You will need to have Google Chrome installed on your computer. Using other web browsers might result in not being able to load important pages such as Workplace and Task Console.

Always create Chrome profiles to keep different accounts - like work and personal or those of other members in your household - on your computer separate. With such profiles, you can keep all your Chrome info such as bookmarks, history, passwords and other settings separate. For guidance, check here.

Workplace

What is Workplace?
Workplace is our proprietary project management platform and is the main portal which you will be using. This is where:
  • You are able to view and request to join available projects.
  • You are being allocated to projects.
  • You are able to access project guide and work file once you're accepted to the project.
  • You are able to view your earnings, ratings, your unlocked project types and many more.
Need more help? Email SupaAgent Hub.
What do I need to do if pages on Workplace are not loading?
Check your internet connection and try to refresh your page. If the issue persists, drop a message in the #tech-issues Slack channel. The following information would be helpful for us to solve the issue:
  • Screenshot of what you see (with the URL & timestamp would be better).
  • Your SupaAgent ID.
  • Project ID (if applicable).
  • Explain the steps you did before reaching the error.

Task Console

What is SupaAnnotator and SupaMiner?
Task console is our platform that allows project managers to design and distribute microtasks to SupaAgents at scale without development time. We currently have SupaAnnotator and SupaMiner as working platforms on Task Console.

Check out this video for a quick summary of our working platforms.

SupaAnnotator:
This is Supahands’ answer to Computer Vision. It is an image annotation tool which you can use to label objects in an image. The labelled images are fed to a computer, which will learn to recognize those objects based on the labelled images.

SupaMiner:
This is our data management platform that distributes microtasks to be completed by SupaAgents. The work or data created is usually compiled and used to train Artificial Intelligence.

Depending on the type of task to be executed, you will work on Workplace using SupaMiner or SupaAnnotator. Sometimes you will need to work directly on the client’s platform.

Need more help? Email SupaAgent Hub.

SupaAgent Training Beta

What is SupaAgent Training Beta?
It's a training feature where you can train yourself on SupaAnnotator platform by practicing image annotation tasks.

Need more help? Email SupaAgent Hub.
Will I be paid for doing annotations on SupaAgent Training Beta?
No. All annotations done in SupaAgent Training Beta [Image Annotation] are not payable and strictly for training purposes only.

Need more help? Email SupaAgent Hub.
How do I access this training?
  1. Log in to Workplace.
  2. Navigate to New Projects section, and click View All.
  3. Search for SupaAgent Training Beta [Image Annotation], or click this link.
  4. Click the 'Request to Join' button. You will be given access to the project guide and training images within 3 business days.
  5. Once given the access, SupaAgent Training Beta will appear in My Projects > Pending Commit tab. Click the 'Commit' button.
  6. Read the SoP/written guideline and you can start training!
Need more help? Email SupaAgent Hub.
How many images should I complete?
It's up to you! Feel free to try as many images as you like, cross-check your answers with the Answer Key, and stop once you feel confident and familiar with the platform and the type of task. To exit, click the 'Exit and End' button.

Where can I find the Answer Key?
The link to the Answer Key document is provided on the last page of the SoP/written guideline.

Recommendations

Wellbeing and productivity tips
To maintain your wellbeing and to increase your productivity, we’ve listed a couple of tips.

Ergonomics:
Computer ergonomics are very important as working at a computer for prolonged periods of time can be harmful to your overall health. Adjust your chair and desk height so your feet are flat to the floor and your arms and thighs are parallel to the ground. Make sure your spine, back, arms and wrists get proper support. Sitting behind your desk for long hours is not good for your health, try alternating between having a standing desk or adjustable-height desk as it can bring you many benefits. Place monitor 20-40” from your eyes and away from the glare. Top of the monitor should level with or below your eyes.

Light:
Be surrounded by natural light as it improves mood, focus and sleep. Sound:
Sounds such as background music may increase your creativity, while noise like other people’s conversations in the coffee shop or the neighbour’s dog barking may distract you. Use a pair of noise-cancelling headphones to drown out distractions and increase your productivity.

Temperature:
Be in an environment between 20-25 degrees Celsius (68 to 77 degrees Fahrenheit) as it may improve your accuracy and productivity.

Even if your situation doesn’t allow you to follow up on all these suggestions, it’s still good to be aware of how such can affect your health and performance.

If you’re keen on more information on how to maintain your wellbeing, do check out the Maintaining Your Physical, Social & Mental Wellness as a Gig Worker article.

What is Project Satisfaction Survey?
Project Satisfaction Survey helps us measure how satisfied you are with the projects you’ve been assigned to (and worked on). Therefore you may receive email(s) with a link to a survey that should only take 1 minute of your time.

You are required to fill up your SupaAgent ID, which is only visible to members of the SupaAgent Hub, thus rest assured that your submission will remain anonymous from Project Managers. Should a Project Manager have any questions or a need to clarify on your feedback provided, then the SupaAgent Hub will always ask for your permission to give away your details.

What is SupaAgent Net Promoter Score?
We care about your overall experience as a SupaAgent. Every quarter you may receive an email to a 1-minute survey on how likely you are to recommend SupaAgent to your friends or family.

Filling up your SupaAgent email address is optional. However, providing your email address helps us better understand your feedback for further improvement.
