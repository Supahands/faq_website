Workplace Profile

Last reviewed: 14 July 2020

Profile

What do I need to do if Workplace does not recognize me when logging in?
Make sure you have received an email granting you access to Workplace. If you still can't log in, this might be because your browser is trying to log in with your default Gmail account. Try the following steps:

  1. Log out of all current Google accounts.
  2. Log in to your Gmail account that you used to register as a SupaAgent.
  3. Go to Workplace and try logging in again.
If the issue persists, drop a message in the #tech-issues channel on Slack or send an email to SupaAgent Hub and our team shall assist you.
Can I change the email account I’ve registered with Supahands?
No, at this point it is not possible to change the Gmail account that you used to register as a SupaAgent, unless it is required for technical reasons.

Need more help? Email SupaAgent Hub.
How can I fill in or edit my profile on Workplace?
Follow these steps to fill in or edit your Workplace profile:
  1. Log in to Workplace.
  2. Go to your profile picture at the top right corner.
  3. Click Edit Profile button.
  4. Complete these details:
    • Personal Information
    • Contact Information
    • Education and Experience
  5. Click Save button.
Note: To change your banking information, refer to this article [TO FILL IN].

Need more help? Email SupaAgent Hub.

Referral

What is a project queue?
A project queue is a new way of joining a project on Workplace. Each SupaAgent who joins a project queue will be assigned a tutorial slot. Every project queue has an allocated number of tutorial slots. Once the queue is full, no more tutorial slots will be assigned. For further information please refer to our Supahands Project Queue terms and conditions . If you have any questions about the process, please submit a request.
What is a tutorial timeslot?
A tutorial timeslot refers to the start time allocated for you to take a project tutorial. It is your responsibility to make sure you are on time for your tutorial. If you are late, the system will only hold your tutorial slot for an additional 10 minutes. After this grace period, you will not be able to take the project tutorial.

Why do I need to wait in a queue to join a project?
To access the Project Guide:
We want to make sure everyone has a fair shot at working online with Supahands. To help with that, we require every SupaAgent to join a queue and wait in line for a tutorial slot.
Will I get onto a project if I join a queue?
No, joining a project queue does not guarantee a position in a project. You will still need to pass the project tutorial or assessment in the allocated time frame.
