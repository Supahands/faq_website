Payment Process

What's the payment process like?
After you’ve done work for a project, the Project Manager will do a Quality Control (QC) and upload a Timesheet for your approved work. Example: If you have done work in April, then you will be able to see your Timesheet on Workplace at the beginning of May; the payment month will be April and you will receive the payment in May. Then we will do our monthly cross-checking and pass the accountants the amounts to be transferred to your bank account. [Angela to insert diagram]

Need more help? Email SupaAgent Hub.
What do I need to do to get paid?
In order to get paid you need to:

  • Have done work that has been approved
  • Have a minimum payment of at least USD 5.00
  • Make sure your payment details are correct on Workplace"


Need more help? Email SupaAgent Hub.
When will I receive my payment?
"You will be paid within ten Supahands business days of every month for the previous month. However, please note that bank holidays in your country might cause a delay. If you have a non-Malaysian bank account, our third-party service provider Bloom or Xendit shall send you an email to notify you when the transfer is in progress.

Be assured that we’ll always pay you for work that you have done successfully!

Need more help? Email SupaAgent Hub.
How will I know the total of my earnings for the month?
If you have done some work during the month, you will receive an email from the SupaAgent Hub after the payment calculations have been finalised.

Please note that if you have a non-Malaysian bank account, our third-party service provider Bloom or Xendit will also send you an email to notify you when the transfer is in progress.

Need more help? Email SupaAgent Hub.
Why did my earnings disappear from my Workplace homepage?
Every first day of the month, Workplace resets the earnings display. Don't worry, your earnings are still recorded and you will be paid according to the final timesheets. You can check your earnings history through the following steps:

  1. Log in to Workplace
  2. Click on your profile picture at the top right corner
  3. Click My Earnings


Need more help? Email SupaAgent Hub.
Is there a minimum payment threshold?
Yes, due to transaction fees, we can only process payments that are above USD 5.00. If your payment amount for the month falls below this, they will be brought over to the next month and added in with the next month's payment. So don't worry, you'll always get paid.

Need more help? Email SupaAgent Hub.
Will I receive my monthly payment per project (separately) or per multiple projects (combined)?
The monthly payment for all projects will be combined in one single payment.

Need more help? Email SupaAgent Hub.
What are the payment methods for receiving my earnings?
  • Bank account (Malaysia, Philippines, Indonesia, and Vietnam)
  • Cash pickup (Philippines)
  • PayPal


Need more help? Email SupaAgent Hub.
Where can I find the payment method details where my earnings will be sent?
Your selected payment method is displayed in My Earnings section on Workplace homepage. If you want to see the details, follow these steps:

  • Log in to Workplace
  • Click on your name below the Supahands logo
  • On the Profile tab, scroll down to the Banking Information section.


Should these details be incorrect or should these be updated, please send an email to supaagent.hub@supaagents.com.

Need more help? Email SupaAgent Hub.

How do I change my payment method?
In case the payment method details as on your profile on Workplace need to be changed, send an email to supaagent.hub@supahands.com before the last day of the month.

For PayPal, make sure the PayPal account is the same with your registered SupaAgent email address.

For Bank Transfer, provide the following in the email:
SupaAgent ID:
Bank Name:
Bank Address:
Bank Branch:
Bank Country:
Bank Account Number:
Account Holder Name:

The changes will be visible on Workplace within 1-2 working days after we receive all the required information.

Need more help? Email SupaAgent Hub.
What is the cutoff date for my new payment method to be captured for payment?
The last day of the month. For example, for January SupaPayments, you can still update your bank details on the 31st of January. Any updates after that will only take effect in the next payment cycle.

Need more help? Email SupaAgent Hub.
I have not received my payment. What do I do?
Firstly, make sure you have a minimum payment of at least USD 5.00. If you still have not received your earnings after the 10th Supahands Business Day, please contact supaagent.hub@supahands.com If it's due to your PayPal or bank account being closed, there will be a fee to redirect your earnings.

Need more help? Email SupaAgent Hub.
Which payment method would Supahands recommend?
We highly recommend using bank transfer. PayPal may charge you for receiving earnings from us, while Cash Pickup (for Philippines only) require you to collect the earnings within 1 month. Should you fail to do so an additional charge will be deducted to reactivate the transaction.

Need more help? Email SupaAgent Hub.
Where can I ask a question regarding the payments?
Firstly, check the latest communications in the #supapayments channel on Slack as the answer to your question might just be there. If you can’t find the answer to your question, please post your query there or send an email to supaagent.hub@supahands.com and our team shall assist you.
